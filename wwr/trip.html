Apurimac River Rafting Black Canyon 3 Days

Description

The Apurimac river (Speaker of the Gods) is the most direct source of the Amazon River, making it the longest river in the world. It has different sections, some ideal for rafting. This river is rated among the 10 best rafting trips globally. The trip starts in Cusco at 3,400 meters altitude, descending to 2,200 meters, where the weather is warmer.
